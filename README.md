# Tata Motors Financial Audit (FY16-FY25)

### 🔍 Objective
A comprehensive financial health assessment of Tata Motors, focusing on the growth trajectory of the Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) segment and the domestic EV expansion strategy to derive actionable investment insights.

### 🛠 Tools Used
- Advanced Excel (Financial Modeling, Ratio Analysis)
- Corporate Financial Statements & Annual Reports
- PowerPoint (Analyst-Grade Deck)

### 💡 Key Insights
- Evaluated debt-to-equity ratios and operational efficiency of the JLR segment.
- Analyzed capital expenditure allocation toward Electric Vehicle (EV) infrastructure.
- Provided a strategic forecast based on multi-year historical financial performance.
